MeowPassword makes strong passwords you can actually remember — built from a
database of over a thousand cat names, scored for complexity, and copied with a
tap. Then it does something no other password app does: MeowGram lets you hide a
secret message inside a cat picture and send it.

PASSWORDS
• Generate secure passwords from cat names, with adjustable numbers, symbols,
  and length.
• Complexity scoring picks the strongest candidate for you.
• Analyze any password to see how it holds up.

MEOWGRAM — SECRET CAT-MAIL
• Pick one of 100 cats, type a secret message, and share it — the message is
  hidden invisibly inside the image.
• Optional passphrase locks the message so only your recipient can read it.
• Send straight from the Messages app, or decode a received MeowGram right from
  the share sheet.

Private by design. Your passwords and messages never leave your device except
when you choose to send them.
