Two superpowers, one very cool cat

A password generator and a secret cat-mail studio — with a game-show attitude.

GENERATE!

Sayable strong passwords

Five candidates conjured from 1,000+ cat names, scored by Kolmogorov-style complexity. The highest score wins. Tap to copy.

JUDGE!

Honest strength verdict

Paste any password and get a 0–10 score with a cheeky verdict — from "a kitten could paw this open" to "fur-midably complex."

Tuck a secret note invisibly inside an ordinary-looking cat photo using DCT steganography. Lock it with a passphrase (ChaCha20-Poly1305) if you like.

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. The Android build ships without the network permission at all. Your cats keep your secrets.
